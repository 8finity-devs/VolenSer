About us

Throughout our history, we’ve worked toward a single purpose: engaging communities through volunteer action. VolenSer got its start when our founder realized that we could better serve our community by creating the best possible volunteer experience. For more than 5 years, our organization has seen succsfull stories .

When we were founded in 2016 , we had no idea that we could make this differnce to the public.

Over the years, the one thing that’s always stayed the same is the commitment and work jointly with the team .

Each year has been an opportunity to grow.

Since our founding, the unwavering dedication of volunteers helped our mission come to life.

We wouldn’t be who we are today without them and key milestones that shaped our programs and services.
Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission is to help create the best possible volunteer experience.

Our vision is to create engaged communities through volunteer action.

Specifically, we shall:

  • Be recognized as a ‘centre of excellence’ for volunteer services and support
  • Develop innovative approaches to volunteering
  • Bring leading-edge products and services to our city
  • Develop professionalism, collaboration and competence among our staff!
  • Be recognized for our high level social and community impact
Our Values
  • Volunteering contributes to the vitality and capacity building of the community
  • Volunteers and the professionals that support them are an integral component of the voluntary sector
  • Honesty, integrity and fiscal responsibility are demonstrated in all areas of our work
  • Diversity of community stakeholders are respected through the model of Cultural Competence and is reflected in policies and service delivery
  • We believe sharing of experience and knowledge is the cornerstone of community and leadership development
